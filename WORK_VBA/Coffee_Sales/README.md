# ☕ Coffee Sales Dashboard — Executive Summary Report

## Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of coffee shop sales performance, focusing on revenue, quantity sold, product categories, store locations, time-of-day trends, and monthly growth. The objective is to identify key revenue drivers, customer purchasing patterns, and opportunities for operational and strategic improvement.

---

## Sales Performance Summary
The business shows strong overall performance with **$504,343.03 in total revenue** from **214,470 units sold**, across **9 product categories**. The **average revenue per transaction is $4.69**, indicating a steady balance between volume and pricing.

---

## Product & Category Analysis
**Coffee is the top-performing category**, leading in both total revenue and quantity sold, making it the primary revenue driver. **Tea** ranks second, confirming strong demand for beverage-based products.  
Lower-performing categories such as **Packaged Chocolate and Branded items** contribute minimally to sales, suggesting limited customer interest or weaker product positioning.

At the product level, **Sustainably Grown Organic Coffee and Dark Chocolate beverages** generate the highest revenue, highlighting customer preference for premium and specialty offerings.

---

## Store Location Performance
Among all locations, **Astoria generates the highest total revenue**, followed by **Hell’s Kitchen**, while **Lower Manhattan** shows comparatively lower performance. This suggests differences in foot traffic, customer demographics, or operational efficiency across locations.

---

## Time-Based Sales Trends
Sales are highly time-dependent, with **peak demand occurring between 9 AM and 11 AM**, particularly at **10 AM**, driven largely by coffee purchases.  
Evening hours show significantly lower sales volume, indicating reduced customer activity later in the day.

Monthly analysis reveals **consistent growth from January through June**, demonstrating increasing demand and positive business momentum in the first half of the year.

---

## Key Business Insights
- Morning hours are critical for revenue generation.
- Beverage categories significantly outperform packaged goods.
- Premium and organic products deliver high revenue impact.
- Location performance varies notably and presents optimization opportunities.

---

## Strategic Recommendations
- Optimize **staffing, inventory, and production during morning peak hours**.
- Expand and promote **high-performing coffee and tea products**.
- Reassess **low-performing categories** through bundling, pricing adjustments, or discontinuation.
- Leverage best practices from **Astoria** to uplift performance in other locations.
- Implement **targeted promotions during off-peak hours** to drive incremental sales.

---

## Conclusion
The Coffee Sales Dashboard highlights a strong, growing business driven by morning beverage consumption and premium product offerings. By aligning operations with peak demand and optimizing underperforming areas, the business can further increase efficiency, revenue, and customer satisfaction.
