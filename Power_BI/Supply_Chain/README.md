## Conclusion

### Revenue Optimization
- Focus on improving sales of lower-performing SKUs through marketing or promotions.
- Reduce defect rates in cosmetics to minimize revenue loss.

### Transportation Efficiency
- Investigate and resolve pending inspections, especially for Rail transportation.
- Improve pass rates for Air and Sea inspections.

### Supplier Management
- Collaborate with low-performing suppliers to increase their revenue contribution.
- Review distribution strategies for optimal use of all transportation modes.

### Customer Engagement
- Investigate why "Unknown" demographics place the most orders.
- Strategize marketing to target underperforming customer groups effectively.
